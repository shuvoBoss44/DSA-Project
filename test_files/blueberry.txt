1: Blueberries are small, round berries with a deep blue or purple color.
2: They are often referred to as a superfood due to their high antioxidant content.
3: Blueberries can improve memory and cognitive function.
4: They are native to North America and grow on shrubs.
5: The berries are versatile and can be added to pancakes, muffins, and yogurt.
