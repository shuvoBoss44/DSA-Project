1: Barcelona is the capital and largest city of the autonomous community of Catalonia in Spain.
2: It is known for its art and architecture, notably the fantastical Sagrada Familia church.
3: The city was designed with a unique grid pattern known as the Eixample.
4: Barcelona hosted the 1992 Summer Olympics, which revitalized the city.
5: It is a major cultural, economic, and financial center in southwestern Europe.
