1: Istanbul is a major city in Turkey that straddles Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait.
2: Its Old City reflects cultural influences of the many empires that once ruled here.
3: The Hagia Sophia features a soaring 6th-century dome and rare Christian mosaics.
4: Istanbul is the largest city in Europe by population within city limits.
5: The Grand Bazaar is one of the oldest and largest covered markets in the world.
