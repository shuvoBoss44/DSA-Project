1: A Power Supply Unit, or PSU, converts mains AC to low-voltage regulated DC power for the internal components of a computer.
2: It is a critical component, as a failing PSU can damage other parts of the system.
3: PSUs are rated by their wattage output and energy efficiency.
4: Modular power supplies allow users to attach only the cables they need, improving airflow.
5: 80 Plus is a popular certification program that rates the efficiency of PSUs.
